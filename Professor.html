Specialization and Research Interests

Precisely optical analysis techniques for semiconductors and low-dimensional materials
High-efficiency photo-electron conversion devices
Eco-friendly energy harvestings
Light harvesting and light extraction
Thermal management with near-zero energy consumption
Nanofabrications and nanoscale (DUV and EUV) lithography
Characterizations and applications of nanomaterials
Day-time radiative cooling
Optical thin-films
Eco-friendly devices, sensors, and materials
