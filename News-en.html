2019 / 7 Out laboratory conducted innovative research on the directional behavior and modulation of hot electrons generated by surface plasmons using magnetic fields. The study, "Magnetic Fields Affect Hot Electrons in Silicon-Based Photodetectors at Telecommunication Wavelengths," was published in July 2019 in Materials Horizons, an influential journal in the materials science field by the Royal Society of Chemistry (RSC). It was also selected for the back cover feature of the journal. This research was completed by several students from the laboratory, including Lin Tzu-Yao(林子堯), Lin Keng-Te(林耕德) , Lin Ching-Che(林敬哲), Li You-Wei(李祐維) , Hsu Long-Tai(徐隆泰) , and Chen Wei-You(陳韋佑).
